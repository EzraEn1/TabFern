TabFern

Google Chrome extension for saving and restoring sets of tabs, and for switching between windows and tabs from a vertical, grouped list.

View on GitHub Add to Chrome

    TabFern privacy policy

    TabFern collects window and tab titles, URLs, and favicon URLs in order to do what it is supposed to do! All information is stored only locally, on your computer. The TabFern extension does not send any data to any party.

    Google may collect information when you install or uninstall TabFern, but TabFern itself does not report those activities or otherwise collect data about usage. If you like TabFern, please star it on GitHub!
